    • 1. RSW Understanding Document

    Retail Servicing Workflow used to develop a paperless image and workflow system to replace the current paper based environment. The solution would facilitate all steps in the process to include imaging, indexing, workflow management, and document storage.The RSW application is implemented to streamline the delivery of mail items, speed up the processing and turnaround time of mail items via work queues and tracking metrics/reporting of the process.
